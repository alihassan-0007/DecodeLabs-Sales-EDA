# 📊 DecodeLabs Sales Data Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of the DecodeLabs sales dataset. The objective was to clean, validate, and analyze the data to uncover meaningful business insights related to sales performance, customer acquisition channels, and product profitability.

The analysis focused on transforming raw sales data into actionable insights that can support strategic business decisions and improve overall performance.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Assess and improve data quality through systematic cleaning and validation.
* Analyze sales trends and revenue patterns.
* Evaluate product performance and revenue contribution.
* Examine customer acquisition channels and their effectiveness.
* Identify anomalies and outliers within the dataset.
* Generate actionable business insights through data visualization.

---

## 🔍 Data Preparation & Cleaning

To ensure reliable analysis, several data quality checks and preprocessing steps were performed:

* Identified and addressed missing values.
* Removed duplicate records.
* Standardized category and product naming conventions.
* Validated data consistency across fields.
* Corrected formatting issues and data entry inconsistencies.

---

## 📈 Key Findings

### Revenue Concentration Analysis

A small group of products generated a significant portion of total revenue, demonstrating a clear Pareto (80/20) pattern in sales performance.

### Customer Acquisition Insights

Referral source analysis revealed which channels contributed the highest number of conversions and generated the most revenue.

### Outlier Detection

Several high-value transactions were identified that deviated from normal purchasing behavior, presenting opportunities for targeted marketing and customer segmentation.

### Product Performance Evaluation

Top-performing products were identified based on sales volume and revenue contribution, highlighting key drivers of business growth.
